CELEBRATING BLACK HISTORY - Jet (USA) - February 28, 1994 - page 9
February 28, 1994 | Jet (USA) | Volume 85 | Issue 0021-5996 | Page 9

Civil Rights Activist Rosa Parks (r), joins Carritos Elementary School students Marcia Fernandez (l) and Wyann
Vaughn, during a Black History celebration in Glendale, CA. Parks, who made history in 1955 when she
refused to give up her bus seat to a White man, addressed the student body. Parks also recently celebrated
her 81st birthday.
